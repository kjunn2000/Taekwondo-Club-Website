History

History Of A.P.U Taekwondo Club

The A.P.U Taekwondo Club has trained continuously in Taekwondo since 2010. The club is established for the purpose of adding another dimension to the school’s diversity by sharing and performing martial arts skills to promote physical conditioning and self-defense, and to attain mental and physical balance. Our current coach is Master Tony.

Mission
We exist to help students get the most from their student experience. We believe that an experience led by students, and informed by the student voice, delivers the biggest benefits for both students and society.

Vision
Our vision is for every student to get the most they can out of their time at the A.P.U University.

Our members range from students of the University’s four undergraduate colleges and various graduate schools to alumni. We are athletes of all belt levels. We are students of all majors, individuals with a vast range of interests. We are a team brought together by our passion for the art and sport of Taekwondo. No matter who you are, no matter where you're from, if you have an interest in Taekwondo, you belong with us. We are Asia Pacific University Taekwondo. Here is a short video regarding our club.
