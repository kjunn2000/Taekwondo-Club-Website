About Us

Annyeong-haseyo~Our club is open to all A.P.U. students who are interested to know more about Taekwondo.




Who are we?

The APU Taekwondo Club is one of the famous club in APU . The aim of the club is to teach Taekwondo to every person who wishes to join, enjoy great fun and get fit. Whether you are beginner or expert, and independently of sex, age or physical condition, Taekwondo will give you the boost you are looking for. The course focuses on traditional and modern styles of Taekwondo, be it Poomsae, self-defence or fighting. At all times, everybody's safety is our priority.
