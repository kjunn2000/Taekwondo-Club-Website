FAQ Section

Do i need to pay any fee for joining the club?

Yes. Our member need to pay rm30 for coach fee per month.

How to join APU Taekwondo Club?

You are able to join our group by registering online or go to the admin office which is located at the 4th floor of APU.

How do i check my training schedule?

Training schedule will be sent to you through email every week. If you dindt receive any email from us, please visit contact us.

Where should i go to for my training?

Our training is located at the free zone next to the auditorium5 on 3rd floor.

Is it compulsary to attend the training.

Yes, any abscent should be given a acceptable reason.
