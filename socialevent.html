Social Event

We hold a series of Taekwon-Do related social events through the year to give members and opportunity to bond and celebrate achievements and milestones.

1 / 4
Christmas Celebration
2 / 4
Mid Autumn Celebration
3 / 4
Chinese New Year Celebration

Deepavali Celebration

    During the festival, the league members gather together to celebrate and share the cultural customs of different ethnic groups, regardless of race or international community.Such a move promoted everyone's feelings, every celebration is very warm and happy.We also welcome you to join us and let us know more about the festival.

    Our club will hold some social activities together with other universities' taekwondo clubs to share learning experience, exchange skills and establish friendship, so that students can know more friends who are interested in taekwondo and increase their interest in learning.

    Social events are an important element of our activities to encourage friendships, break down social barriers and to rightfully acknowledge the significant contributions of our members.

    We will also held welcoming party to welcome our new member,play games,having dinner together, let new members integrate into the club faster
